SPARCS

SPARCS: System Programmers' Association for Researching Computer Systems is the best computer R&D club in KAIST. We make services for KAIST members from 1991. We recruit new members at the beginning of every spring and fall semester.

Services
  • ARA - Official KAIST Community
  • OTL - Online Timeplanner with Lectures!
  • Zabo - Upload and Share Posters on the Internet
  • SSO - Single Login for the SPARCS Services!
